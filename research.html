Research Interests:

My research interests lie broadly in topology, geometry, and mathematical physics. I'm in the process of expanding my horizons and exploring different subtopics. My experience in knot theory has led me to be interested in alternating knots, Vassiliev invariants, sliceness of knots, and knot diagrams.
